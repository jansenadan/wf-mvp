Find your Size in 2 Quick Steps!

Underbust

90% of bra support is in the band, which lays under your bust.

  • Pull a measuring tape tight
  • Level around your ribcage, just under your bust
  • Do not round to the next whole number, get an accurate reading.
22½ 36½

Overbust

With your current bra on, measure over your bust.

  • Stand straight and take a good, deep breath
  • Pull the tape loosely around your ribcage
  • Over the fullest part of your bust
  • Make sure the tape is level around your body.
29 46½

Exchange & Returns

Yes, you can! And we want to make it really easy for you. We will fully refund the cost of returned items if you make sure that:

Can I exchange my items?

If the size doesn't fit you and you want to exchange for a different one, we will be happy to offer you bra fitting advice and free size exchange. Please ensure that:

For the orders placed from the US and Canada, the returns should be made to the US. Orders placed from other countries should be returned to Poland. While returning the items, please ensure that the package is visibly marked on the envelope as RETURNED GOODS.
